Projects

COVID-19 FACE MASK DETECTOR

Building on my fascination with computer vision, I investigated COVID-19 mask compliance and defined a neural network based on a ResNet-50 model to construct a face mask detector that can effectively differentiate between masked and unmasked individuals.

ResumAI

ResumAI is a resume template website for UC Berkeley students that uses ML models to provide personalized feedback on resumes. Technologies used include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React for front-end, MongoDB for back-end, and Sk-learn for machine learning.

Alzheimer's Drug Discovery

I participated in the AI4ALL program where I worked with UC Berkeley AI researchers to develop an autoregressive model that learns from data of existing molecules to expedite drug development and predict IC50 values for efficient Alzheimer's treatment discovery.
